About Us

ERamen shop is a renowned and prestigious ramen shop that has been serving customers for over a century. Established in 1910 in Japan, it has since become a household name in the world of ramen. The shop has a rich history and a strong tradition of using only the finest ingredients to create their delectable ramen dishes.

In 2015, ERamen made its way to Indonesia, bringing with it a taste of Japanese culture and cuisine. Since then, the shop has become a popular destination for food enthusiasts, both locals and tourists alike, seeking authentic Japanese ramen noodles.
