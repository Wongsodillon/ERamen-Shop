About Us

Our History
Our ERamen restaurant was founded in 1910 in Tokyo, Japan. Within a few years, we managed to gain popularity and good reputation among ramen noodle fans in Japan. In 2015, we opened our first branch in Indonesia and since then it has become one of the best ramen noodle restaurants in Indonesia. Continuing our legacy of authentic Japanese flavors.
Our Mission
Our vision at ERamen is to become the best ramen noodle restaurant in Indonesia that continues to grow and provide added value to customers and the surrounding community. We strive to create a unique dining experience by combining traditional flavors with innovative twists, ensuring that every bowl of ramen is a delight for our customers.
Our Mission
Our mission at ERamen is to serve high-quality, authentic Japanese ramen noodle dishes with great service. We always try to present a satisfying dining experience and satisfy customers with the dishes served. Our commitment is to sourcing finest ingredients and making unrivaled taste in every bowl of ramen.
